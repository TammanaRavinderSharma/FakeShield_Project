    How FakeShield Works

    🔍

    Identity Analysis

    Username entropy, digit ratios, bot-pattern regex matching, display name quality, disposable email domain detection.

    📊

    Activity Heuristics

    Account age, posting frequency, follower/following ratio analysis, LinkedIn connection depth.

    🧠

    NLP Content Scan

    Spam keyword detection in bio & posts, sentiment variance analysis (flat sentiment = bot indicator), duplicate post detection.

    🤖

    ML Layer

    Random Forest classifier trained on synthetic profiles blends with heuristic scores to produce the final fake probability.

    Results are probabilistic — always combine with human judgment for high-stakes decisions.
